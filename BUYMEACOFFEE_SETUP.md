# Buy Me a Coffee Setup Guide ‚òï

This guide will help you set up your Buy Me a Coffee account so people can support your project.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [https://www.buymeacoffee.com](https://www.buymeacoffee.com)
2. Click "Sign up" or "Start my page"
3. Choose a username (this will be your URL: `buymeacoffee.com/yourname`)
   - Recommended: Use `frankwiersma` to match the GitHub username
4. Sign up with email, Google, or another option
5. Verify your email address

## Step 2: Customize Your Page

1. **Profile Settings**
   - Upload a profile picture
   - Write a bio (e.g., "Developer | Creating useful tools and extensions")
   - Add your location (optional)

2. **Page Content**
   - Add a cover image (optional but recommended)
   - Write your main message:
     ```
     Hey! üëã

     I create free, open-source tools like LinkedIn Post Vault.

     If you find my work helpful, consider buying me a coffee!
     Your support helps me continue building useful tools for the community.
     ```

3. **Set Your Goal** (optional)
   - You can set monthly supporter goals
   - Or leave it open-ended

## Step 3: Payment Setup

1. Click on "Settings" ‚Üí "Payments"
2. Choose your payment method:
   - **Stripe** (recommended - available worldwide)
   - **PayPal** (available in many countries)
3. Connect your account:
   - Enter your bank details or PayPal email
   - Complete verification
4. Set your coffee price (default is $5, but you can change it)

## Step 4: Update Your Links

Once your page is live, update these files:

### 1. Update README.md
